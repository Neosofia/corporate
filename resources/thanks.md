# Thanks

It takes a village to build anything worthwhile. Neosofia's platform, design, and thinking have all been shaped by the generosity of people who share their knowledge openly. This page is our small way of saying thank you.

## Josh W Comeau

[Josh's blog](https://www.joshwcomeau.com/) is a masterclass in how to communicate complex technical ideas with clarity and delight. His interactive writing style, thoughtful typography, and the care he puts into every detail of his site directly inspired the look and feel of this one — from the dark palette and callout boxes to the general philosophy of making technical content approachable.

> [!NOTE]
> Imitation is the sincerest form of flattery. We hope we've learned well.

If you work in web development and haven't read his blog, fix that immediately.
