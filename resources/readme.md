## Our Policies and Procedures

We've taken a unique approach to implementing all of our policies and procedures. Specifically:

* They're free for you to copy and use as a template for your organization
* They're managed using git for version control
* When paired with [Neosofia Docs](https://github.com/Neosofia/docs), you can generate auditor-friendly PDF documents that include content sections and formatting such as:
  * A title page with a control ID, company name/logo, version, effective date, and disclaimer
  * A table of contents
  * headers and footers with page numbering, company name, section information, and document status
  * A changelog with the version number, when it was changed, who changed it, and why it was changed
  * Electronic signatures for both the author and approver (reviewer signatures are still a WIP)
