# Neosofia

Neo and Sofia are the Greek words for new and wisdom. As a technology services company, our mission is to create new wisdom in the compliance space to give organizations the tools and knowledge needed to deliver safe and effective services to their clients.

## About

This repository contains the Neosofia corporate website and related company site content, including the app, markdown pages, and supporting documentation. It is the source for the company web presence and the docs that support our public-facing policies.
